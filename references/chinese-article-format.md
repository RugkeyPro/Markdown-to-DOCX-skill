# Chinese Article DOCX Format

Apply all text in black.

## Titles

- Chinese title: SimSun/宋体, 二号/22 pt, bold, centered.
- English title: Times New Roman, 小二/18 pt, bold, centered.

## Abstracts And Keywords

- Chinese abstract title `摘 要`: Chinese font SimSun/宋体, English font Times New Roman, 小二/18 pt, bold, centered, spacing before 0.5 line and after 1.5 lines.
- Chinese abstract body: SimSun/宋体, 小四/12 pt, fixed 22 pt line spacing, spacing before 0 and after 0, first-line indent 2 characters.
- Chinese keyword label and keywords: label SimSun/宋体, 小四/12 pt, bold; keyword text SimSun/宋体, 小四/12 pt; spacing before 1 line and after 0.
- English abstract body: Times New Roman, 小四/12 pt, left aligned, fixed 22 pt line spacing, first-line indent 1 character, spacing before 0 and after 0.
- English keyword label and keywords: label Times New Roman, 小四/12 pt, bold; keyword text Times New Roman, 小四/12 pt, left aligned, spacing before 1 line and after 0.

## Table Of Contents

- TOC title: SimSun/宋体, 小二/18 pt, bold, centered, single line spacing, spacing before 0.5 line and after 1.5 lines.
- TOC entries: SimSun/宋体, 小四/12 pt, fixed 22 pt line spacing.

## Headings

- Level 1 heading: SimHei/黑体, 三号/16 pt, bold, centered, spacing before 1 line and after 1 line.
- Level 2 heading: SimHei/黑体, 小四/12 pt, bold, left aligned, spacing before 0.5 line and after 0.5 line.
- Level 3 heading: SimSun/宋体, 小四/12 pt, bold, left aligned, spacing before 0.5 line and after 0.5 line.

## Body

- Body: Chinese font SimSun/宋体, English font Times New Roman, 小四/12 pt, left aligned, first-line indent 2 characters, fixed 22 pt line spacing.

## References

- Reference entries: Chinese font SimSun/宋体, English font Times New Roman, 五号/10.5 pt, left aligned, fixed 20 pt line spacing, hanging indent 2.5 characters, spacing before 0 and after 0.

## Captions And Tables

- Figure captions: below the figure, centered, SimSun/宋体, 五号/10.5 pt, single line spacing, spacing before 0.5 line and after 0.5 line.
- Table captions: centered, SimSun/宋体, 五号/10.5 pt, single line spacing, spacing before 0.5 line and after 0.5 line.
- Text inside tables: single line spacing, spacing before 0.25 line and after 0.25 line.
